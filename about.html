Company Overview

Freelancer.com is the world's largest freelancing and crowdsourcing marketplace by number
of users and projects. We connect over 72,483,951 employers and freelancers globally from
over 247 countries, regions and territories. Through our marketplace, employers can hire
freelancers to do work in areas such as software development, writing, data entry and design
right through to engineering, the sciences, sales and marketing, accounting and legal
services.
Freelancer Limited is trading on the Australian Securities Exchange under the ticker ASX:FLN

Provenance
Freelancer.com has acquired several outsourcing marketplaces including GetAFreelancer.com and EUFreelance.com (founded by
Magnus Tibell in 2004, Sweden), LimeExchange (a former business of Lime Labs LLC, USA), Scriptlance.com (founded by Rene
Trescases in 2001, Canada, one of the early pioneers in freelancing), Freelancer.de Booking Center (Germany), Freelancer.co.uk
(United Kingdom), Webmaster-talk.com (USA), a forum for webmasters, Rent-A-Coder and vWorker (founded by Ian Ippolito, USA,
another early innovator in the freelance marketplace space)
